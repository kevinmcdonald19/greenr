About
Facing Ahead is an quiz created by S&K Publishers for couples considering a long-term commitment like marriage. The questions are meant to bring up topics for conversation that will help these couples understand what some of the large issues that face lifelong relationships can be, and how they might approach them.

There are no "right" or "wrong" answers in this quiz; it is meant to compare the two individuals' responses with each other, to show areas of agreement and areas that may need to be discussed or dealt with before a commitment like marriage is considered.

S&K Publishers

S&K Publishers is run by Steve and Kathy Beirne. They have been actively involved in marriage preparation and enrichment for more than 30 years.

Steve has an M.A. in catechetical theology and has been an educator his whole life, beginning as a high school teacher and then moving into adult education. Kathy received an M.A. in child and family studies from the University of Missouri. In addition to her work in marriage education, she also teaches child development in a local community college.

They have written one book and many magazine articles on marriage and family, and have given talks and workshops across the country. They married in 1967, have seven children, two sons-in-law and five grandchildren, and live in Portland, Maine.
